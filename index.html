About

Here is a little background

Hey 👋 I am an AI & Optimization researcher and data engineer, currently a Ph.D. candidate in Industrial & Systems Engineering at Wayne State University and an AI/ML Engineer at General Motors, based in Royal Oak, Michigan.

My research bridges deep learning and operations research to solve real-world optimization problems. I build machine learning pipelines, large language model applications, and sensor-integrated predictive systems — with a focus on attention-based architectures, MLOps, and time-series modeling across manufacturing, energy, healthcare, and supply-chain domains.

My dissertation explores “Data-Centric and Attention-Based Architectures for Learning and Solving Optimization Problems.”
